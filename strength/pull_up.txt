
PULL UP
This is best done on an overhang, the steeper the overhang, the harder this will be. You should do this task for every single move on the route
LEVEL 1: touch your chin to your highest hand
LEVEL 2: touch your chin to your highest hand and let go with your lowest hand
LEVEL 3: touch your chin to your highest hand and take your feet off of the wall while you lower your weight back down 
LEVEL 4: take both feet off of the wall then tough your chin to your highest hand
LEVEL 5: take both feet off the wall, touch your chin to your highest hand and hold it there for 3 seconds
