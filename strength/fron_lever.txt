
FRONT LEVER
This is best done on a roof or fingerboard with dead space underneath. It is more important to look for good form than number of reps. Ensure you engage your shoulders
LEVEL 1: bring your legs into a pike position and do a shoulder shrug
LEVEL 2: bring your knees to your chest and try to rotate backward until your feet touch the beam
LEVEL 3: touch feet to the beam then lower into an assisted front lever (person or resistance band
LEVEL 4: touch feet to beam and lower to a one legged front lever 
LEVEL 5: raise and lower in a front leve
